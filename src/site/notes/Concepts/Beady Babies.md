Beady Babies are a collectable plush toy series manufactured by the Kry Corporation.


*"Lately, you have taken a liking to the NOVELTY PLUSH ANIMALS known as BEADY BABIES produced by the Kry Corporation. The most valuable BEADY BABIES in your vast collection will stay stuck behind their designated plexiglass cubes for all of eternity. Trapped in the closet, relegated to the completely arbitrary weals and whims of the RAPIDLY-CHANGING PLUSHIE ECONOMY composed of, almost exclusively, FIFTY- TO SIXTY-YEAR OLD WOMEN and REPULSIVE SCALPERS."*
